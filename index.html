Account Deletion – SiberKoza

This page explains how users of SiberKoza, developed by Taha Khan, can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to connect@siberkoza.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address or username used to register in the app

We may contact you to verify account ownership before processing the request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Profile data
  • App activity and preferences
  • Any data associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

  • Transaction or billing records (if required by law)
  • Security or fraud-prevention logs

Retention Period

Any data that must be retained is stored for a maximum of 30 days, after which it is permanently deleted.

If you have any questions, please contact us at connect@siberkoza.com.
